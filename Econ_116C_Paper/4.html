2.2 Mobile phone modem market
Wireless communication in fact has a lot of different standards with the improvements
from generation to generation. In China, the most common used second-generation (2G)
standards include GSM and CDMA, third-generation (3G) standards include WCDMA,
CDMA2000, and TD-SCDMA, and fourth-generation (4G) standards include LTE-TDD and
LTE-FDD. Different generations of wireless standards are considered complementary to ensure
smooth upgradation, and it is necessary for network operators to have compatible switches from
one certain generation to a corresponding other. The market of modems also include the market
of SOCs, since OEMs are wishing to purchase more components from one distributor in order to
decrease the overall size of its final product. In both situations, Qualcomm is the significant
market leader with high consumer satisfaction.
