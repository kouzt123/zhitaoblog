5.0 Limitations
This paper comes with certain limitations. First, it was still far from gathering enough
evidence to further prove its claim. Since the case was relatively new and government decisions
were made privately, there were much more information yet to reveal. Second, the paper did not
have enough room to discuss the relationship between Qualcomm Chinese case and all other
case, which might lead to a more reliable result. In the future, as there will be more information
to be released, it is possible to conclude an entirely different result.
6.0 Conclusion
This paper talked about how Qualcomm Inc. generates its revenue and conduct business
advantages. By analyzing these facts, the paper examined the Chinese NDRC decisions and
believed that the NDRC decisions have reasonable claims while lack certain evidences in some
parts. The overall fine was not high and had similar functions of a business trade-off. In short,
the limitation of Chinese government made the Qualcomm case a reasonable reference but not
100% reliable to determine future lawsuits.
