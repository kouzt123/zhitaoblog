The main argument between Qualcomm and other companies is whether or not the 5%
“Qualcomm tax” is legitimate. It’s charges its price based on the selling price of the product,
which means that a portion of the technologies that have no relations with Qualcomm are also
charged. Similar arguments can be found in Apple Inc. statements, saying “(Qualcomm) has
nothing do with the display or the Touch ID or a gazillion other innovations that Apple has
done”
