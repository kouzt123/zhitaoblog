3.5 NDRC decisions
After the fourteen month investigation, NDRC officially published its decision in 2015.
Key solutions include
:
19
1. In the process of licensing, Qualcomm has to provide a full list of patents without any
expired patents.
2. Qualcomm has to respect the willingness of the OEMs and should not require to
license patents back to Qualcomm without a price.
3. Qualcomm’s patent will no longer calculated as a percentage of the total selling price.
Instead, the price is calculated as 5% of the 65% of the selling price of a CDMA-included phone
and 3,5% of the 65% for a LTE-only phone.
4. Qualcomm cannot force OEMs to sign SEPs and non-SEPs as a required bundle.
Further negotiating is necessary for both parties to determine what patens should be included.
5. Qualcomm cannot force a “no patent no chips” policy without the right from OEMs to
challenge the terms.
