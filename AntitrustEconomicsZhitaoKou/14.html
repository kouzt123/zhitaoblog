4.2 Has Qualcomm abused its dominance?

Article 17, it lists 7 actions which are violations of the law. The two violations that are
mentioned to Qualcomm case are part 1 and part 5, saying it is illegal to sell at an unfair high
price and illegal to sell with unfair condition.
